[try these simple patterns]

Simple Triangle

 Materials:
  • 3 90cm embrodery floss red
  • 3 90cm embrodery floss white
  • scissors
  • clipboard/tape
 Steps:
  1. Cut all the pieces of thread, you can make them smaller depending on your wrist size
  2. Tie a knot about 1.5-2 inches down one end. Then braid the shorter end, with another knot at the end
  3. Then fasten this end of the bracelet some place. I like to take a clipboard and stick the bracelet end into the hook, or you can simply take a piece of tape and tape the end to your workplace
  4. Then with the thrird left string make a forwards, backwards knot onto the next string
  5. Then from the left make forward knots until the last one of that color
  6. Then from the right make backward knots until the last one of that color
  7. Then make a backwards forwards knot onto the next string
  8. Continue this pattern until the desired length
  9. If you get lost just remember you're making triangle shapes with each color

Beaded Braid

 Materials:
  • 3 40cm embroidery floss
  • scissors
  • 15-20 beads
  • clipboard/tape
 Steps:
  1. Cut the pieces of thread, if you have thicker beads or want a thinker bracelet you can double up on the string
  2. Gather the string and tie a knot at the very end of the strings
  3. Fasten the bracelet to a clipboard or piece of tape
  4. For about 1-1.5 inches braid the strands like a normal 3 strand braid
  5. Then after each braid slip a bead on to the middle string
  6. Then continue braiding like normal, make sure not to braid too tight around the beads or they might pop out rather than the embedded look
  7. Continue this for the desired length then end with another knot

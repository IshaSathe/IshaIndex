Garlic Naan

 Ingedients:
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 3/4 cup milk (or water)
  • 3 tbs greek yogurt
  • 2 tbs salted butter (or cocunut oil)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • chopped fresh clinatro
 Steps:
  1. In a large bowl mix all the dry ingredients then add the olive oil, milk, and Greek yougurt and mix until the dough comes together.
    If the dough is too try add more milk or if it's too dry add some flour.
  2. Set the dough aside in a lightly oiled bowl. Let it sit for 30 minutes at room temperature, covered.
  3. After the dough has sat, seperate the dough into six equal pieces. On a lighly floured rolling board, roll out each piece into an oval shape about 1/8 inch thick.
  4. Next, grate the garlic. In a saucepan, heat the butter and garlic over medium eat, cook for one to two minutes until the garlic starts to yellow (but not brown). Remove from heat.
  5. Heat a cast iron skillet on high. Add a rolled out piece to the head and cook until the top starts to bubble and the bottom is browning (about one minute), then flip and cook for another thirty seconds.
  6. Brush each piece in garlic butter and sprinkle chopped cilantro, serve warm.
  7. Enjoy!

Vada

 Ingredients:
  • 1 cup ragda
  • 4 mini samosas
  • 1 tsp tamrind chutney
  • 2 tbs chopped onion
  • 2 tbs diced tomato
  • 1 pinch red chili powder
  • 1 pinch cumin poweder
  • 1 pinch chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp finely chopped coriander leaves
 Steps:
  1. First heat the samosas, these can either be homemade left overs or frozen ones.
  2. Next take the ragda and pour it over the samosa, be sure to cover the whole samosa or it will not fully absorb the flavor
  3. Mix in the onion and tomato
  4. Top with tamrind chutney and green chutney
  5. Sprinkle chili powder, cumin powder and chaat masala
  6. If desired as a small scoop of curd for a slightly tangy flavor
  7. Enjoy!

Jalapeno Mozzerlla Sticks

 Ingredients:
  • 1 cup planko bread crumbs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic poweder
  • 8 sticks mozzerlla string cheese
  • 8 small jalapenos
  • 16 wonton wrappers
  • bowl of water
  • 1 egg
  • oil for frying
 Steps:
  1. Combine the panko, salt and garlic powder together in a bowl
  2. Plae one piece of mozzerlla in one half of a jalapeno and wrap both with a wonton wrapper. Use the water to fold the wrapper around the mozzarella so it's closed.'
  3. Dip into the egg and cover with panko mixture to coat completely
  4. Repeat with the rest
  5. Place in a an air frier for about 15 minutes
  6. Enjoy!

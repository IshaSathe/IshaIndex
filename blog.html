no more blacktop

This is one of the stories that I tell all the time. While it may not seem the funniest at first it's pretty for me to look back at. So, in 2nd grade we had some extra recess time and so my teacher thought we'd play sharks and minos and so while running back and fourth I somehow tripped which is still a mystery because I was wearing velcro sneakers becuase I didnt know how to tie my shoes! And when I got taken to the nurse I didn't tell her how bad it hurt and she just assumed it was a scape so when I came in the next day with a cast everyone was so confused. Looking back at it, I laugh about how shy I was that I couldn't tell the nurse that my arm was broken!!

5 random facts

1. Avacados are a fruit, not a vegtable. They're actually a single-seeded berry.
2. The Eiffel Tower can be 15 cm taller during the summer. It's called thermal extension, the particals gain kinetic energy and take up more space.
3. Australia is wider than the moon. The moons diameter is 3400km while Australia is 4000km wide.
4. 'Mellifluous' is a sound that is pleasingly smooth and musical to hear.
